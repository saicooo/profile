About Me

Hello! My name is Mahmoud Hanafy, and I am a Front-end Developer and UI Designer with 5 years of experience crafting beautiful, functional, and user-centric web applications.

My passion lies at the intersection of design and code, ensuring that every project is not just aesthetically pleasing but also technically robust and highly performant. I specialize in modern JavaScript frameworks (like React and Angular) and robust utility-first CSS frameworks (like Tailwind).
